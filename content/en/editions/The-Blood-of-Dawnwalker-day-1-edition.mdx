## FAQ

**Q: Is The Blood of Dawnwalker day 1 edition the same as Collector’s Edition?**

A: No. The Day 1 Edition is the first-print physical Standard version with steelbook and map. Collector’s adds premium items like the statue and physical world guide, plus digital Eclipse content.

**Q: Does pre-ordering give exclusive gear forever?**

A: Pre-order buyers get early unlock access to the Sangora Traveler armor set, but the set is still obtainable later through normal gameplay.

**Q: Is the Day 1 content guaranteed worldwide?**

A: Not always. Retail descriptions indicate possible regional variation. Always check your local retailer’s exact item list before paying.

**Q: Should I buy digital Eclipse instead of Day 1 Edition?**

A: Choose Eclipse if you value digital lore extras most. Choose Day 1 if you want physical bonuses like the steelbook and map without stepping up to full Collector’s pricing.
