## Managing Character Progression

The engine tracks your narrative choices via a "redemption" variable. Every time you cross a line or kill on a whim, the game world shifts accordingly. These technical markers influence which NPCs are willing to assist you in the rebellion against Brentis. You cannot simply rely on brute force; you must manage your reputation and your internal corruption levels as defined by the game's logic gates.

> **💡 Tip:** Pay attention to the dialogue cues. If an NPC mentions your "dormant" nature, you are likely approaching a threshold where your Varkyr abilities will become more potent, but your social interactions will become more strained.

## FAQ

**Q: Does The Blood of Dawnwalker engine support ultrawide monitors?**

A: Yes, the engine natively supports ultrawide resolutions. However, you may need to adjust the Field of View (FOV) slider in the settings menu to prevent visual distortion during cutscenes.

**Q: How does the engine handle stealth in the game?**

A: The engine uses a light-value detection system. If your character’s visibility percentage is above 30%, enemies will likely spot you if you enter their line of sight. Using the environment to block light sources is the most effective way to remain undetected.

**Q: Can I change my graphics settings mid-game?**

A: Most graphical settings can be adjusted in real-time, though certain high-end textures may require a quick reload of the current area to apply correctly.

**Q: Is the Varkyr transformation permanent?**

A: No, the transformation is tied to the engine's day/night cycle. Once the sun rises, the Varkyr traits will naturally fade, returning your character to their human state.
