## Core Systems You Should Understand Before Buying

The game’s open-world loop is easy to misread at first glance. Exploration itself does not appear to consume your timeline directly; completing major objectives is what advances time. That distinction is huge.

### 1) The 30-day progression structure  
You’re operating under a soft but meaningful deadline. The design seems intended to make decisions matter rather than force speedrunning every quest.

### 2) Day vs night form identity  
By day, you’re more human-focused with swordsmanship and learned magic.  
By night, vampiric mobility and predatory tools become central, including wolf-form traversal.

### 3) Corruption and vampiric growth  
Feeding increases corruption, and corruption appears tied to access to stronger vampiric perks. Expect moral and tactical tradeoffs.

### 4) Infamy and enemy response  
As you disrupt vampiric operations and clear hostile zones, your infamy rises. Higher infamy may trigger new edicts from enemy leadership, making travel and encounters harder.
