The game structure uses a **30-day progression frame** for major objectives. Current details suggest that completing objectives drives time forward more than free exploration. That distinction is critical: it encourages intentional quest sequencing rather than random completion.

### Day vs. night identity

During daytime, your kit leans into human capabilities like swordsmanship and learned magic paths. At night, vampiric mobility and predatory options become central, including enhanced traversal and aggressive engagement tools.

Power gain appears tied to feeding behavior, with corruption influencing access to vampiric perks. This system may create an ethical and strategic tension: short-term combat strength vs. how corruption may shape your broader role-play outcomes.
