## Seamless Narrative and Combat Integration

A major technical hurdle for the development team was moving away from traditional "paused" cutscenes. In earlier builds, activating abilities from the radial menu would trigger a cinematic moment, effectively pausing the game. However, based on community feedback, the developers have shifted to a seamless system.

This change has a direct impact on how **The Blood of Dawnwalker voice actors** recorded their lines. Instead of isolated studio sessions for cutscenes, many lines are now delivered dynamically during gameplay. This "flow-state" combat ensures that the pacing remains high, with the game merely slowing down rather than stopping when players access their abilities.

### Combat Voice Mechanics

The introduction of "Omni attacks" and "Omni blocks" also required a robust set of vocal cues. These mechanics allow players who prefer a more action-oriented experience to engage with combat without needing to focus heavily on directional inputs.
