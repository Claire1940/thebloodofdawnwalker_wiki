## Build Planning: Early, Mid, and Late Game Priorities

The most common mistake with **the blood of dawnwalker vampire clans** is spreading points too early. You’ll usually get better results by building one reliable loop first.

### Stage 1 (Opening Hours): Establish One Kill Loop

Pick one repeatable pattern, such as:

1. Engage tool (dash, mark, charm, silence)  
2. Burst window (stagger, bleed, detonation, command)  
3. Sustain trigger (feed, shield, cooldown refund)  
4. Exit or reset (blink, cloak, fear, reposition)  

At this stage, consistency beats style.

### Stage 2 (Midgame): Add Utility, Not Noise

Once your main loop works, add only one extra layer:

- Anti-ranged answer  
- Anti-elite tool  
- Emergency disengage  
- Crowd split/control option  

### Stage 3 (Late Build): Expand Identity
