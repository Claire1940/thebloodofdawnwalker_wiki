Bakir is depicted as a destructive war-born personality, likely from steppe campaigns, with a taste for fear as spectacle. This kind of character tends to enforce rule through public violence and reputation.

Ambrose appears to be the youngest vampire among the major names. Newly empowered antagonists are often narratively unstable—high risk, high ego, and eager to prove themselves through excess.
