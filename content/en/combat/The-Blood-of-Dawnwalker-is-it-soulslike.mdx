## Combat, Difficulty, and Build Identity in 2026

Combat may be the biggest deciding factor for you. If you only play pure Soulslikes, you should focus on these revealed mechanics before committing.

### 1) Directional combat over pure mash combos
The combat presentation suggests deliberate attack direction and timing instead of pure spam chains. That can feel closer to tactical melee RPGs than to arcade action.

### 2) Human and vampire forms are not cosmetic
Human and vampire modes have different move sets and utility. You may solve the same objective with distinct risk profiles depending on form and time of day.

### 3) Skill-wheel slowdown keeps pressure alive
The skill interface appears to slow time rather than fully pause it. That keeps decisions tense and reduces “menu safety,” which many action fans prefer.
