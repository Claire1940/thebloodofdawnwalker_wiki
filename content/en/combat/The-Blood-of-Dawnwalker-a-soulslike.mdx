## Build Planning Framework for Day-One Players (If It Is Soulslike)

If **The Blood of Dawnwalker a soulslike** turns out accurate, your early progression choices will matter a lot. Even without confirmed stat trees, you can follow a universal launch-week framework:

### Step-by-Step Launch Build Strategy

1. **Choose one combat identity early**
   - Example identities: evasive duelist, guarded bruiser, hybrid spellblade.
2. **Invest in survivability before damage spikes**
   - HP, mitigation, and stamina efficiency usually stabilize learning.
